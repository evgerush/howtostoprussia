NO SWIFT for Russian

Complete blocking of SWIFT: a strike on the economy and reputation of Russia

The situation in Ukraine is becoming tenser every day. Russian troops are firing on the homes of civilians, destroying the infrastructure of cities, bombing schools and hospitals, and the number of victims among the civilian population is steadily growing.
Taking this into account, measures taken by European countries to stop Putin's aggression seem completely insufficient. Yes, thanks to international sanctions and restrictions, the economy of the Russian Federation has been hit hard, and its decline is already reflected in the damage it has done to all Russian residents. But not everyone has yet realized how much this will affect every citizen.
Now is the time to tighten the economic noose even tighter!
Ukraine insists on a complete blocking of the SWIFT system for Russia. Such a move will send Russia's banking system back to the stone age, all financial flow will slow drastically and become much more expensive. It will damage all areas of Russian business and cause irreparable damage to the Russian Federation's reputation. Previously, such sanctions have only been instituted against Iran and notorious North Korea. Like those nations, Russia will automatically become an outcast in the entire world community. All countries of the world will refuse to cooperate with representatives of Russian business.
For Ukraine, blocking Russia from SWIFT is a vital lever of influence against the Russian government, whose brutality sees no boundaries at all.

This is also an opportunity for Russian business representatives to rise up against Putin's murderous regime.
